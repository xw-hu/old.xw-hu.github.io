Biography

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng and Prof. Chi-Wing Fu. Before that, I received the B. Eng. degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2016. I worked with Prof. Xuemiao Xu for undergraduate research.

My research interests cover computer vision and deep learning, with special emphasis on low-level vision. Specifically, I am dedicated to designing learning frameworks by exploring the physical properties of various vision applications, including shadow image analysis, rain removal, and saliency detection. I am also interested in exploring robust learning algorithms with less human supervision for image recognition and restoration, which helps to improve scene understanding and enhance camera capability.
